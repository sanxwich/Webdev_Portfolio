What is Kawaii Recipes?

Kawaii Recipes is a webpage where I store all my homecook recipes. Instead of writing a recipe down in a note or making a document. I decided to keep all my recipes in one website with functionality and cutesy theme design from Sanrio and Kawaaii Count.

This website is a personal project of mine that helps me in sharpening my web development skills not just the design but on how my recipe website will work for me to share my homemade recipes to other people who wishes to see. The website will be built uisng react framework, css, javascript, and html.

What I would improve and add

As of now im still in the design stage on how this website will be structured and designed for users to enjoy. I would improve on the functionality such as the recipe buttons and guide button to make the recipe.
